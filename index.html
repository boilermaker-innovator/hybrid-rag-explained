How It Works

One JSON config → full interactive widget

🎯

Customer chooses options

Materials, styles, access conditions — tappable cards, not dropdown menus

📏

Sets dimensions with sliders

Length, height, quantity — drag to set, see the price move in real time

💡

Learns through the "i" button

Why colorbond costs more. Why slopes add cost. Contextual education, not walls of text

💰

Gets a live estimate

Ballpark range updates as they go — no more "how much roughly?"

📬

Submits a structured enquiry

Every spec attached. Ready to quote — not "hi I need a fence how much?"

⚡

Tradie quotes in minutes, not days

No back-and-forth. No phone tag. Decision happens on the spot
